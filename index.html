Selected Works:

Several “second-screen” companion websites for National Geographic Channel:

Live From Space – National Geographic Channel

Emmy-award winning companion site for the live TV program of the same name. Site plots the position of the ISS in real time while using a variety of data APIs to reveal similarities and differences between people across the globe. Social extension featured participation of both Neil deGrasse Tyson and Stephen Hawking.

Killing Kennedy – National Geographic Channel

A unique, immersive take on the assassination of JFK that compares the paths of President Kennedy and Lee Harvey Oswald at critical moments in their lives. The project was a prolific winner of awards, including six Cannes Lions for categories including Storytelling, Overall Aesthetic, User Experience and Mobile.

Saints and Strangers – National Geographic Channel

Re-framed the story of the Pilgrims as a game that challenges players to face the life-or-death decisions required to survive a first winter in Plymouth.

Tech-driven ideas for Royal Caribbean to fulfill their mission of “marketing as innovative as the ships”:

SoundSeeker – Royal Caribbean

AI-powered site experience that uses machine learning to create custom, shareable photo soundtracks which are generated on the fly based on objects, colors and facial expressions found in user submitted images.

SeaSeeker – Royal Caribbean

Social content series that brought Snapchat Spectacles underwater for the first time with a custom designed 3D-printed dive mask enclosure.

Come Seek Live – Royal Caribbean

Live streamed Periscope content from remote locations in the Caribbean to digital billboards in NYC using a proprietary video processing pipeline.

VR Prototype – Royal Caribbean

Concepted and created a prototype for an ambitious VR experiential installation that used capacitive touch technology to add interaction to real world objects in a virtual reality space, transporting users to unique Caribbean experiences.

Get Away With It – JetBlue

Live streaming game show, twenty-five episodes long, with contestants connected to our studio set via Skype. Responsible for technical and creative elements as well as creating and testing the show’s gameplay design.

Freedom Crown – Burger King

Instagram social content for Burger King, created custom crown with space for an American Brewhouse King burger and a Bud, for a collaboration with Budweiser.
